The big decision

Old regime or new?
Here's what it actually means.

We walk you through both — and help you calculate which one saves you more money based on your actual situation.

Old Regime
More deductions, more work
Tax slabs5%, 20%, 30%
80C deductionsYes — up to ₹1.5L
HRA exemptionYes
Standard deduction₹50,000
Best forHigh investments & rent
Worth it only if your total deductions exceed ₹3.75L. Otherwise, new regime likely saves more.
New Regime
Lower rates, simpler filing
Tax slabs5%, 10%, 15%, 20%, 30%
80C deductionsNot available
HRA exemptionNot available
Standard deduction₹75,000
Best forFreshers, low investments
Default from FY 2024–25. Most freshers and early-career earners benefit more from this regime.
